What is Frozen Pizza?

Ah yes, frozen pizza. If you can't make ampalaya or adobo, then rather than giving up and starving to death you can make a frozen pizza! Frozen pizza is pizza prepared in a factory which makes it convenient to prepare. High in calories and not as tasty, but knock back a few shots of whiskey and frozen pizza might turn into a super gourmet and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Frozen pizza

Let's start cooking!

  1. Preheat the oven based on the manufacturers instruction
  2. Unwrap the pizza from the packaging and place in the oven
  3. Let the pizza cook per the manufacturers instructions
  4. Take it out when ready
  5. Let the pizza cool down for a bit so you don't burn you mouth
  6. 食べましょう！
