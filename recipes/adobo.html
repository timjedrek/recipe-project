What is Adobo?

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish that can be prepared with minimal ingredients. Adobo is a very popular dish and when people think of Filipino food, they think of Adobo. Let's make some adobo!

Ingredients

  • Meat of your choice (usually prepared with chicken, pork, beef, or squid
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Optional: Potatoes
  • Optional: Carrots

Let's start cooking!

  1. Prepare the ingredients first. Cut meat into peices with your own discretion. If using potatoes and carrots, cut those also to an appropriate size. Cut onions and mince garlic.
  2. Put oil in a pan and cook at medium heat
  3. Once oil is ready, put the onions and garlic into the pan. Stir occasionally until the onions are caramelized.
  4. Add meat and stir occasionally.
  5. Once meat is fully cooked, add soy sauce and vinegar. Soy sauce makes the dish more salty and vinegar makes it more tart. Add the appropriate amount to your liking.
  6. If you want to add potatoes and carrots, add them know and wait about 5 minutes that way the heat and steam can soften the vegetables.
  7. That's it! Enjoy your newly prepared Adobo dish!
